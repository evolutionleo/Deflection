Attacks:

\/ Phase 1 - Normal
  1) \/ The boss shoots laser into the player
  2) \/ The boss shoots 3 waves of slow bullets in all directions
  3) \/ The boss shoots a "shotgun" wave of bullets into the player.
     The bullets are fast, but can be parried
Phase 2 - Enraged
  1) \/ The boss shoots laser into the player, then it slowly rotates to follow him
  2) \/ The boss shoots 6 waves of fast bullets in all directions
  3) \/ The boss shoots 6 super-fast bullets in a row 
Phase 3 - Fan
  1) The boss is constantly rotating and shooting bullets in 4 directions.
